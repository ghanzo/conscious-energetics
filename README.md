# Conscious Energetics

A book exploring energy as the fundamental substrate of reality — from cosmic origins through biological life, human civilization, and the evolution of consciousness.

## Structure

The book is organized in three parts, each reflecting a dimension of energy:

- **Part I: Creation** — What energy is, how it appears, how it behaves
- **Part II: Interaction** — How energy flows through life, civilization, and power
- **Part III: Existence** — Consciousness, agency, and what we are becoming
